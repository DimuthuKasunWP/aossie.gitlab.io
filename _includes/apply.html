How to Apply

Follow the steps below to become a GSoC student in one of our projects.

  • Read
    the
    Docs

    Before You Start

    Read all the official GSoC documentation carefully. Read the Frequently Asked Questions and their answers. Familiarize yourself with the programme's timeline and do not miss deadlines.

  • Explore
    our
    Repos

    Choose a Project

    Visit the gitlab repositories of our projects and find a project that you like. Some projects may contain a "GoogleSummerOfCode.md" file with project specific GSoC instructions. Follow the instructions there, if applicable.

  • Write
    your
    Proposal

    Expand an Idea

    You should choose one of the ideas suggested here and your proposal should expand that idea, giving details of how you plan to implement that idea. Follow the proposal template.

  • Talk
    to
    Us

    Use our Forum

    At any moment, if you have questions, just ask us. Introduce yourself in our forum. Write a draft of your proposal early, to allows sufficient time to give you feedback.


  • Apply!
